Write 0xA500 to the CPU-WDT RSTCSRW register.

For writing to WTCSRW and WTCNTW, use 16-bit writes only:

*(volatile uint16_t *)WTCSRW = 0xA518 | (value & 0xFF);
*(volatile uint16_t *)WTCNTW = 0x5A00 | (value & 0xFF);
